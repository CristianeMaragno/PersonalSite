About me

Hello, I'm Cristiane and my journey with programming starts in 2016, when I started the mechatronics course, since then I have been interested and involved with several technologies. Initially, I learned C and C ++ for microcontroller programming (like Arduino) with the objective of participating in national and international robotics competitions(RoboCup 2017, that happened in Nagoya-Japan). Later, I acquired knowledge about web development technologies(HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP and MySQL) that I used to work as a freelancer. Android development also piqued my interest (Java, Kotlin and Firebase) and more recently I have been trying to delve deeper into Machine Learning (Python, Pandas, Matplotlib and Sciki Learn). I am a naturally curious person who likes challenges and that always seeks to improve.
